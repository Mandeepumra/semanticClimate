1. What do we mean by development pathways?
37
In the present report, development pathways refer to patterns of development resulting from multiple
38
decisions and choices made by many actors in the national and global contexts. Each society whether
39
in the Global North or the Global South follows its own pattern of development (Figure 1.6).
40
Development pathways can also be described at smaller scales (e.g., for regions or cities). By extension,
41
the concept can also be applied to sectors and systems (e.g., the development pathway of the agricultural
42
sector or of industrial systems).
