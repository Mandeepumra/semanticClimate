Box 4.4 India’s national development plan
19
India’s initial national development plans focused on improving the living standards of its people,
20
increasing national income and food self-sufficiency. Accordingly, there was a thrust towards
21
enhancing productivity of the agricultural and industrial sectors. While the main focus was on
22
maintaining high economic growth and industrial productivity, poverty eradication, employment and
23
inclusive growth remained important priorities. The National Action Plan on Climate Change with 8
24

25
considerations in planning and decision making

th

28

29

30

31

33
