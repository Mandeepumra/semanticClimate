30

31

32

33

34

In sum, this Chapter suggests that the immediate tasks are to broaden and deepen mitigation in
41
the near-term if the global community is to deliver emission reductions at the scale required to keep
42
temperature well below 2°C and pursue efforts at 1.5°C. Deepening mitigation means more rapid
43
decarbonisation. Shifting development pathways to increased sustainability (SDPS) broadens the scope
44
of mitigation. Putting the enabling conditions above in place supports both. Depending on context, some
45
enabling conditions such as shifting behaviour may take time to establish, underscoring the importance
46
of early action. Other enabling conditions, such as improved access to financing, can be put in place in
47
a relatively short time frame, and can yield results rapidly.
