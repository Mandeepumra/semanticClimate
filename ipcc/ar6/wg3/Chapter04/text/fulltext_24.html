6

7

8

Box 4.1 Adaptation Gap and NDCs
30
NDCs have been an important driver of national adaptation planning, with cascading effects on sectors
31
and sub-national action, especially in developing countries. Yet, only 40 developing countries have
32
quantifiable adaptation targets in their current NDCs; 49 countries include quantifiable targets in their
33
national legislation (UNEP 2018a).
34

35

36

37
